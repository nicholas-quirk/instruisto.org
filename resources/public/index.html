Suffix Review

—aĉ—
disparagement, contempt, dislike, wretched, vile, awful

—ad—
(adds the idea of action) action, particularly prolonged, repeated, or habitual

—aĵ—
denotes concrete idea

—an—
member (of a group), inhabitant (of a country)

—ar—
collection (group, set) of (several or all) units of the same kind

—ebl—
denotes possibility, -able, -ible

—ec—
denotes quality or state (in abstract form), -ship, -ness

—eg—
great in size or degree

—ej—
place (with verb root) where an action occurs

—em—
denotes an inclination, bent, bias, proclivity, leaning, -full

—end—
which-must-be-done

—er—
denotes element or unit of homogeneous whole

—estr—
leader, head, “boss”, ruler, commander, chief

—et—
dimunitive; weakens the idea of the root to a high degree

—id—
offspring of living creatures

—ig—
make, cause to be, render, -ify

—iĝ—
become, come to be, get, grow, turn

—il—
object used to perform an action, appliance, tool, implement, instrument

—in—
denotes female, feminine

—ind—
worthy of, deserving of

—ing—
holder for one object (sheath, socket, stand)

—ism—
doctrine, movement, theory, system, -ism

—ist—
person connected with the root, usually by profession (business, calling, vocation, habitual interest)

—obl—
denotes multiplication, -ple

—on—
denotes fraction, -th

—op—
collective numeral (number of elements together, in a group)

—uj—
container, receptacle, holder (also countries)

—ul—
denotes individual (person)

—um—
no definite meaning: sense is defined by usage or context

—cĵ—
affectionate endings (male)

—nj—
affectionate endings (female)
